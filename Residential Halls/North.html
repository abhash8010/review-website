Review:

Jemi R.
North hall is a very nice place to live. I lived in a 6-person suite and it was an amazing experience! The suites are quite roomy and one bedroom is shared by 2 people. The suites are all same-gendered although males and females can live on the same floor. We had our own kitchen (with a fridge) and bathroom (with two toilets) which was really nice. Another plus was that there were laundry rooms located on each floor. The only gripe was the expensive price. For three terms it cost me over $10k!
8/10
